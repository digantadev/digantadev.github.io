Meet Diganta Das Niloy: A Proud Son of Cumilla, Bangladesh

Welcome to the digital space where we unveil the vibrant persona of Diganta Das Niloy, a promising individual hailing from the picturesque town of Cumilla in Bangladesh.

Early Years and Family Background

Born on January 4, 2002, Diganta Das Niloy entered this world as a beacon of hope and joy to his loving parents, Narayan Chandra Das and Priya Roy. Raised in the warm embrace of Cumilla, a town known for its rich cultural heritage and scenic beauty, Diganta imbibed the values of humility, resilience, and compassion from his surroundings.

Family Ties

In the tapestry of his family, Diganta is adorned with the threads of affection and camaraderie. He shares a special bond with his elder sister, Moitry Das, whose presence adds a touch of laughter and companionship to his journey. Alongside, his younger brother, Joyanta Das, brings moments of mischief and joy, creating a dynamic balance within the household.

to Feb 2023

Diganta Das Niloy's educational journey embodies a relentless pursuit of excellence and a steadfast commitment to academic advancement. Having excelled in his Secondary School Certificate (SSC) examination from Cumilla Zilla School in 2019 and subsequently achieving success in his Higher Secondary Certificate (HSC) examination at Notre Dame College, Dhaka, in 2021, Diganta has consistently demonstrated his intellectual prowess and dedication to learning.
